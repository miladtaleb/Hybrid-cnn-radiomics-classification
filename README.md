# Hybrid-cnn-radiomics-classification
Brain tumors, caused by rapid and uncontrolled cellular proliferation, pose significant health risks worldwide. This study combines Radiomic features with advanced deep learning models, including EfficientNet-B0 and ResNet-18, to classify 2D and 3D brain tumor images effectively.

## Results:
The results demonstrate that our approach effectively distinguishes between Glioblastoma, IDH-wildtype, grade 4, meningiomas, and brain metastases, achieving robust and accurate performance across tumor types. By leveraging both deep feature representations and handcrafted radiomic descriptors, and employing advanced classifiers such as CatBoost and multilayer perceptron (MLP), the proposed method enhances feature interpretability while maintaining strong classification performance. This hybrid strategy offers a reliable, scalable, and clinically applicable solution for automated brain tumor diagnosis.
